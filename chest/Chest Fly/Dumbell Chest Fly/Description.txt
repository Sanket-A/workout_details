Muscles Worked: Chest
Description:
A chest fly is an excellent upper body move that uses dumbbells to strengthen the chest and arms muscles.
The chest fly is unique because it strengthens the chest in a different and complementary way to push-ups or chest press.
For some people, push-ups are not possible due to injuries or limitations.
