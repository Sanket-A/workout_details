Desciption:
The military press is a complete shoulder building exercise perfect for building shoulder muscle. The military press is an exercise with many names and is often referred to as the shoulder press, overhead press, and strict press.
The military press is used primarily to build the deltoid muscle.
It also indirectly targets the other muscles of the shoulder, your triceps, and your core.
Since the military press is completed standing up, it involves a lot of core strength to help stabilize the spine while pressing weight overhead.
